1. Introduction

Noorcat Technology (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a Flask-based web application and uses the WhatsApp Business API to communicate with customers.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with us through WhatsApp or our web application. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp or interact with our application, we may collect:

A. Personal Information

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • WhatsApp profile information (as provided by WhatsApp)
  • Messages and attachments you send to us
  • Any information you voluntarily provide

B. Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Server logs
  • Usage data within our Flask application

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Provide customer support
  • Deliver requested services
  • Send transactional notifications via WhatsApp
  • Improve our application and services
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. WhatsApp Business API

We use the WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. When you communicate with us via WhatsApp:

  • Your messages are processed through WhatsApp’s systems.
  • WhatsApp may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy.
  • We only access and use information necessary to provide our services.

You can review WhatsApp’s privacy policy here: WhatsApp Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely on our servers or trusted hosting providers.
  • We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

However, no electronic transmission or storage system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

You may request deletion of your data as described in Section 8.

7. Sharing of Information

We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate or host our application
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, safety, and property
  • In connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger, acquisition)
We do not share your data for advertising purposes.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Object to certain processing

9. Third-Party Services

Our application may rely on third-party services, including:

  • Meta / WhatsApp Business API
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics tools (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies governing data usage.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
